Always

  • Lead with the benefit
  • Use specific numbers
  • Short sentences
  • Active voice
  • Code as copy
  • One CTA per section

Never

  • Exclamation marks in product copy
  • "Cutting-edge" or "best-in-class"
  • "Empower" or "revolutionize"
  • "Seamless" or "end-to-end"

Boilerplate Copy
Approved descriptions at three lengths. Copy exactly - don't paraphrase.
One-Liner

Mazou is a unified AI gateway for Africa - one API, local currency billing, smart cost routing.

Short - 50 Words

Mazou gives developers and product teams one API to access 40+ AI models - including African language models - with billing in local currency through Paystack. Smart routing automatically selects the best model for each request, cutting costs 15-40% while providing full per-feature spend visibility.

Medium - 100 Words

Mazou is the AI infrastructure layer for Africa. We provide a single, OpenAI-compatible API that connects developers to 40+ AI models - from GPT and Claude to African-built models for Yoruba, Hausa, and Swahili. Teams fund a wallet in naira through Paystack, and our smart routing engine automatically selects the optimal model for each request based on cost, quality, and language requirements. The dashboard shows exactly what every feature, agent, and model costs - with actionable recommendations that save teams 15-40% on average. No USD cards. No vendor lock-in. No black box.
