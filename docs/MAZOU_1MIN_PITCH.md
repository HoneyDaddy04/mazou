# MAZOU — 1 Minute YC Pitch Script

**Delivery:** Calm, direct, confident. No rushing. Every sentence earns the next.

---

## THE SCRIPT

**[0:00–0:10] THE PROBLEM**

"Every company building with AI is now juggling multiple providers — OpenAI, Anthropic, Google — each with its own API, its own pricing, its own dashboard. Nobody knows what they're actually spending, which calls are worth it, and which ones are waste. In Africa, it's worse — you're paying in dollars through cards that get declined, converting at terrible rates, with zero infrastructure to manage any of it."

**[0:10–0:20] WHAT WE BUILT**

"Mazou is one API for every AI model. Global and African. You send requests through us, fund your account in Naira, Cedis, Rand — whatever your local currency is — and our smart routing engine automatically sends each request to the cheapest model that meets your quality threshold."

**[0:20–0:30] HOW IT WORKS**

"The dashboard shows you everything — spend by feature, by agent, by model, by activity type — in real time. And we surface one-click optimisations: downgrade this model, cache these queries, batch those requests. Customers save 15 to 40 percent without changing a line of code."

**[0:30–0:40] WHY IT GETS BIGGER**

"But we're not just an API gateway. As AI agents start needing wallets, web search, commerce — those services plug into the same Mazou layer. Same billing, same visibility. We become the cost control plane for your entire AI stack — the layer you can't rip out because we have your complete spend picture."

**[0:40–0:50] WHY US, WHY NOW**

"AI spend is the new cloud spend — it starts small, grows fast, and one day the CTO looks at the bill and realises most of it was waste. We've seen this exact pattern before in Africa with cloud costs. And right now, there is no infrastructure to manage it. We're building it."

**[0:50–0:60] THE CLOSE**

"Paystack built the payment rails for Africa's internet economy. We're building the infrastructure layer for Africa's AI economy. We're Mazou."

---

## DELIVERY NOTES

- **Total:** 60 seconds at natural speaking pace (~220 words)
- **Tone:** Founder conviction. Not selling — stating facts
- **Key beats to land:**
  - "15 to 40 percent" — pause slightly, let the number land
  - "the layer you can't rip out" — this is the moat statement, say it with weight
  - "We're Mazou" — end clean, no trailing off
- **Do NOT say:** "We're like X for Y" before the Paystack line. Save the analogy for the close where it hits hardest
- **If you only have 30 seconds:** Use the Problem (0:00–0:10) + What We Built (0:10–0:20) + The Close (0:50–0:60)
