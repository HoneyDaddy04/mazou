# MAZOU — 60 Second Video Script

**Format:** Founder direct-to-camera + screen captures of dashboard
**Tone:** Urgent, confident, zero filler. Every sentence earns the next.

---

## THE SCRIPT

**[0:00–0:05] HOOK — Founder to camera, tight frame**

"Every AI call your product makes costs real money. Every single one. And right now, most developers in Africa have no idea what they're spending."

**[0:05–0:15] THE PROBLEM — Quick cuts of API dashboards, billing pages, multiple tabs**

"You're juggling three, four, five different AI providers. Each one has its own API, its own billing, its own pricing model. You're paying in dollars you don't have, through cards that get declined, with zero visibility into which calls are worth it and which ones are burning cash."

**[0:15–0:25] THE SHIFT — Text on screen: TOKENS ARE THE NEW CURRENCY**

"Here's what most people haven't realised yet. Tokens are the new currency. Every agent action, every AI feature, every intelligent interaction — settled in tokens. And the companies that figure out how to manage token flow efficiently will win. The ones that don't will bleed money."

**[0:25–0:40] THE SOLUTION — Mazou dashboard on screen, smooth scroll**

"Mazou. One API, every model — OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, African language models — through a single endpoint. Pay in Naira. Our smart routing engine analyses every request and automatically sends it to the cheapest model that meets your quality bar. Customers are saving 15 to 40 percent on day one."

**[0:40–0:50] THE BIGGER PICTURE — Dashboard showing agent services, wallet balances**

"But we're not just an API gateway. When your agents need wallets, commerce, web search — those plug into the same Mazou dashboard. Same billing. Same visibility. We're building the control plane for the entire AI-native economy in Africa."

**[0:50–0:58] THE CLOSE — Founder back to camera**

"Paystack built the payment rails for Africa's internet economy. We're building the token rails for Africa's AI economy."

**[0:58–1:00] END CARD**

*MAZOU*
*One API. Every model. Pay in Naira.*
*mazou.dev*

- **B-roll needed:** Mazou dashboard screencast, typing code, API response in terminal, Naira wallet funding animation
- **Music:** Lo-fi electronic, subtle build, drops out at 0:50 for the close
- **Captions:** Mandatory — large, bold, 2-3 words per frame for social
- **Aspect ratios:** Film in 16:9, crop to 9:16 for TikTok/Reels/Shorts with text overlays at centre frame
- **Key visual moments:**
  - 0:15 — "TOKENS ARE THE NEW CURRENCY" full-screen text hit
  - 0:25 — Dashboard reveal (hero shot)
  - 0:50 — "Paystack built payment rails..." (founder conviction shot, slightly closer frame)
